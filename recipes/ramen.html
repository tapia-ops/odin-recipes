Pork Ramen Recipe

Designed by Freepik

An Asian soup full of flavor for those days

Ingredients

  • 1 lb: of pork loin
  • Green onions
  • 2 Eggs
  • Beef or Chicken Stock(Optional)
  • Ramen Noodles
  • Mushrooms
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chili oil

Preparation method

  1. To a pot add pork loin, onions, and mushrooms
  2. sauté until golden
  3. To the same pot, add chicken stock or any of your liking
  4. Add ramen noodles and let them cook
  5. In a separate pot boil the eggs for 20 mins
  6. Once eggs are cooked to your liking add them to a container and pour soy sauce
  7. Marinate for 30 min
  8. Add the marinated boiled eggs and chili oil to the ramen and green onions to garnish
  9. Enjoy
