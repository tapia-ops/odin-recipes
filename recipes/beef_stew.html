Beef Stew Recipe

Designed by Freepik

Description

The beef stew recipe is a clasic of the American home using tasty blocks of beef in order to warm the soul.

Ingredients

  • Meat: 2 lbs Beef Chuck Roast or stew meat, cut into 1–2 inch chunks.

  • Vegetables: 4 large carrots (chopped), 1 lb Yukon Gold potatoes (cubed), 1 yellow onion (chopped), 4 ribs celery (chopped), and 3-4 cloves garlic (minced).

  • Thickener & Fat: 3-4 tbsp All-purpose flour (for coating beef), 3 tbsp olive oil, and optional cornstarch slurry.

  • Flavorings: 2 bay leaves, 1 tsp dried thyme or rosemary, 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce, salt, and black pepper.

Key Preparation steps

  1. Coat & Sear: Toss beef with flour, salt, and pepper; brown in a Dutch oven with oil.

  2. Sauté: Add onion, celery, and garlic to the pot.

  3. Simmer: Add broth, wine, tomato paste, herbs, and seared beef. Simmer low and slow (1-1.5 hours).

  4. Finish: Add potatoes and carrots, cooking until tender (20-30 mins).
