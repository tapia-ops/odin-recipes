Rice Pudding Recipe

Designed by Freepik

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon of whole milk

  • 1 can of condensed milk

  • 2 cans of evaporated milk

  • Vanilla extract

  • 3 table spoons of sugar

  • Ground cinnamon

  • Arboreo Rice

Preparation

  1. To a pot Add already washed rice and hav a gallon of whole milk on low heat
  2. stir occasionally
  3. Once rice has softened add 1 can of condensed milk and add the 2 cans of evaporated milk
  4. Let it simmer until milk is warm and some of it has evaporated
  5. Add one tablespoon of vanilla extract and four tablespoons of sugar and stir
  6. Once simmered remove from heat and storage in a fridge to cool down and add cinnamon to your liking
